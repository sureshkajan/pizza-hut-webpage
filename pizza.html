About Catering

A new pizza place is now open at Empire Place in Sioux Falls, behind Chick-fil-A and in front of the Empire Mall. Pizzashop sells pizza by the slice or as a whole, and the fast-casual restaurant menu also includes a variety of Italian dishes such as pasta with homemade meatballs, focaccia sandwiches and calzones.

The store is located at 4013 W. 41st Street and is open for dine-in, takeout and delivery through Doordash. Pizzashop is originally based out of New Jersey. Owner Josiah Urban recently closed his New Jersey store with plans to expand here and in Texas, and he also has another Pizzashop location in Oxford, Mississippi.
